Suleiman FARUK

is an energetic Nigerian youth with a thorough background in technology and innovation. He is a junior back-end developer and an alumnus of the Tiidelab Cohort 3 program. He has worked on various web development projects both on the front-end and on the back-end, and his most recent being Meditech hospital appointment booking system He has worked both as an outbound and inbound online customer service representative for both CNSSL Contact Center Limited and Ison Bpo now Ison Xperience.

In addition, Faruk has experience working as a Sales Executive for Miniso Lifestyle and Zetar Yogurt in Ilorin, Nigeria. He also has experience working as a freelance Graphic Designer and Architect. Having achieved a National Diploma (OND) in Architecture from Federal Polytechnic, Namoda, Zamfara State in 2014, he has since made his life ambition to venture into the technological world and make a difference. He is an excellent team player and a problem solver. His probing mind has turned him into the man he is today and will continue to grow and ask questions.
