Suleiman Faruk,

(othernames: Umar, Adekunle) studied Architectural Technology in Federal Polytechnic Namoda, Zamfara State. He attained a National diploma (ND) in 2014. He is from Oyo state, Originally, and was born in city of Kaduna and lived there until 2015 when he relocated to Kwara State capital of Ilorin for purpose of getting a better job and a new shot at life.

He got his first job in November of 2015 as a Data validation agent in a Telecommunications and outsourcing company named CNSSL Contact Center Limited on a contract basis. The contract ended in August 2016 after which he got another offer as a customer service representative in Ison BPO now known as Ison experience which is also into telecommunications, also an outsourcing company and also on a contract basis The most recent professional and official work experience was as a Sales Executive in Miniso Lifestyle, a company into sales and manufacturing with headquarters in China. The job ended in late 2020 and since then has been working as a graphics designer and technician.

He developed passion for programming in the year 2021. His expertise includes HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, PHOTOSHOP, CORELDRAW, AUTOCAD, REVIT ARCHITECTURE, CLM and many more. He has strong passion for programing and he is willing to go into it as deep as anyone could get. He is the founder of F.arts. and co-founder of Rofut consult.
